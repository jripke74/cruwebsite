CRU SPOKANE

Do Life Together!

A caring community of Christ followers

Cru City Spokane is a community of people who believe in the importance of integrating our personal, professional and spiritual lives.

Cru Spokane comes alongside people on their spiritual journey and offers free resources to help men and women investigate what they believe spiritually and why. We offer one-on-one, mentoring, counsel, study groups and leadership development from a Christian worldview.

OUR STAFF

We have 3 staff members serving with Cru Spokane.

OUR MISSION

The goal of Cru Spokane is to connect people to Jesus and develop spiritual leaders who will advance the mission of Jesus to impact Austin and the world.
